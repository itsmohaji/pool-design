LIJBAILAT POOL — WALKABLE 3D DESIGN STUDIO

1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open index.html in Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox.
3. Desktop: drag to look, WASD/arrows to walk, scroll to zoom.
4. Mobile: use the left joystick to move and drag the right side to look.
5. Use the Design Studio panel to change deck, pool, furniture, wall colour, lighting and atmosphere.
6. “Render Photo” captures the exact current view.

Everything required is included locally; no internet connection or external scripts are needed.
